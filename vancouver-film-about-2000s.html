:: ABOUT VANCOUVERFILMDEPT.COM ::

Welcome to VancouverFilmDept.com - your all-in-one resource hub for the Vancouver film industry! Learn about our mission, our team, and how we're supporting "Hollywood North."

"Our mission is to strengthen Vancouver's film community by providing accessible resources, fostering connections, and supporting the growth of our local industry through shared knowledge and collaboration."

:: OUR STORY ::

VancouverFilmDept.com was created in 2025 by a group of passionate Vancouver film industry professionals who saw the need for a central hub of information and resources. Drawing inspiration from the collaborative spirit of early internet communities, we built this site to help both newcomers and veterans navigate our city's thriving film scene.

Our goal is to break down information silos and make industry knowledge accessible to everyone, regardless of their experience level or connections. By bringing together vendor information, practical tips, discussion forums, and educational resources, we hope to strengthen Vancouver's position as a world-class film production center.

:: FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS ::

How do I get my business listed in the vendor directory?

Contact us at listings@vancouverfilmdept.com with your business information, services offered, and contact details. We review all submissions and update our directory monthly.

Is membership required to use the forum?

Yes, basic registration is required to post in the forum to maintain quality discussions. Registration is free and only requires a valid email address.

How can I contribute tips or resources to the site?

We welcome community contributions! Email us at content@vancouverfilmdept.com with your submission. We're particularly interested in practical tips, production templates, and location insights.

Do you offer advertising opportunities?

We have limited featured vendor spots available. Please contact advertising@vancouverfilmdept.com for rates and availability. We prioritize local businesses directly related to the film industry.

How often is the site updated?

We add new resources weekly, update vendor information monthly, and moderate the forum daily. Our events calendar is updated as new industry events are announced.
